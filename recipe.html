Blueberry Cheesecake Recipe

Ingredients

  1. Graham cracker crumbs: 1 ½ cups (180g)
  2. Unsalted butter: 5 tablespoons (70g), melted
  3. Granulated sugar: 2 tablespoons (25g)
  4. Cream cheese
  5. Granulated sugar: ¾ cup (150g)
  6. Vanilla extract: 1 ½ teaspoons (7.5ml)
  7. Large eggs: 3, at room temperature
  8. All-purpose flour: 2 tablespoons (15g)
  9. Fresh or frozen blueberries: 2 cups (300g), divided
  10. Granulated sugar: ½ cup (100g), divided
  11. Cornstarch: 2 teaspoons (6g)
